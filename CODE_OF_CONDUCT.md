# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

wq-agent should be a practical, respectful place to collaborate. Participants
are expected to:

- Be welcoming to people with different backgrounds and experience levels.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Give actionable feedback and assume good faith when intent is unclear.
- Respect privacy, credentials, and private research material.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal
attacks, publishing private information, or repeatedly disrupting project
spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, limit
participation, or ban contributors when behavior harms the project or its
participants.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainers through a
private channel listed by the project owner. Include relevant links, dates, and
context. Maintainers should handle reports confidentially and avoid conflicts
of interest where possible.
