# `ts_std_dev`

**Category**：Time Series　**Type**：SCALAR

## 签名

```
ts_std_dev(x, d)
```

## 官方说明

Calculates the standard deviation of a data series x over the past d days, measuring how much the values deviate from their mean during that period.
