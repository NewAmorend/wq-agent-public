## 描述

This dataset provides granular financial statement footnote data for publicly traded companies, extracted using advanced XBRL technology. It includes detailed disclosures on items such as goodwill, deferred taxes, share-based compensation, and other key financial metrics not typically found in headline financial tables. By enabling users to drill down into historical and cross-company footnote information, the dataset offers unique insights into accounting policies, risk exposures, and non-standard financial events. Such depth of information can help analysts identify hidden risks, spot trends in corporate behavior, and improve the accuracy of fundamental models for predicting price movements.
